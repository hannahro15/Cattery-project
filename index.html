Welcome to 'The Manchester Cattery!'

This luxury cattery is based next door to the beautiful grounds of Fletcher Moss park in Didsbury, South Manchester.

We pride ourselves in giving our cats the very best of care. If you want the perfect place to take a cat to whilst you are away this place is ideal for you. We have 2 very experienced onsite vets to take care of any health issues with your cats whilst on their stay if necessary. We also have a team of 8 very well trained staff to look after all the cats and to provide a top notch customer service.
